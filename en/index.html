About Mushaar

Who We Are

Mushaar Law and Legal Consultation Firm was established as a professional company dedicated to serving its clients and representing them before judicial and legal institutions according to the best professional and ethical standards.

Through attracting distinguished competencies and specialized work teams, we have become one of the most important legal and Sharia expertise houses in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, and by the grace of God, a success partner for a large number of diverse entities in various sectors (government, private and non-profit).

We strive to achieve our partners' aspirations and meet their desires with efficiency and professionalism, through a spirit of high professionalism and outstanding work quality.

Our Vision

Leadership in legal solutions and excellence in delivering them to the highest international standards, achieving client desires and aspirations

Our Mission

Providing a distinguished legal system that elevates the legal sector locally and globally, with legal competencies that serve the interests of individuals and entities and preserve their rights
